Protect Yourself from Juice Jacking Attacks

What is Juice Jacking?

Juice jacking is a cyberattack that occurs when an attacker tampers with public charging stations or USB ports to install malware or steal data from your device while it's connected for charging.

How to Protect Yourself:

  • Use your own charger and power adapter whenever possible.
  • Avoid using public charging stations in airports, hotels, and other public places.
  • Invest in a portable power bank for on-the-go charging.
  • Consider using a data blocker or USB condom to prevent data exchange when charging in unfamiliar places.
  • Keep your device's operating system and security software up to date.
  • Stay vigilant and report any suspicious charging stations to authorities.

Spread the Word!

Help protect your friends and family from juice jacking attacks by sharing this information with them. Awareness is key to staying safe in today's digital world.
